Playo-About the company

What does Playo do?
Playo is the one-stop sports app to help bring back "play" in our everyday lives. Playo empowers local communities to make new play pals, organize playgroups, share information/experiences and discover sporting venues/activities.
Playo is a singular destination for sports, fitness, fun and all things recreational.
